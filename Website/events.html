''KASH'' Event by Junoon E Pakistan

We have conducted an interactive session with honorable personalities; they represent themselves and tell us about hardships and struggles they have faced during journey of their life. JUNOON PAKISTAN conducts an event “KAASH” at Aiewan e iqbal in Nov, 2019. Our guest speakers were Governor Punjab Chaudhry Muhammad Sarwar, Bilal Akbar Khan, Hina Pervaiz Butt, Rehan Tariq, Jawad Ahmed. Moreover we have different sponsors who made our event successful such as Bykea, Careem, Al basha Food & Management, Adoura, Mughal Steel. We had also two inspirational motivational speakers such as Mudasir Iqbal Shah and Shahwaz Baloch who make our event spectular. Our honorable chief guest represents himself in a very spectular way and shared his life history towards the audience. He shared his views regarding to success that he has achieve in his life and also about dream that he often think to full fill in his life Moreover we had also inspirational personality Rehan Tariq and Jawad Ahmed. Rehan Tariq is a news anchor as well as motivational speaker. He explain his Kassh in a very spectular way towards audience. Jawad Ahmed also shared his story and tell us about the success that he get in his life.

Online Graphic Training Session

Junoon E Pakistan conducted online Graphic Training Session in Pandemic Covi-19. Students learn learning skills by attending online classes on Zoom by the mentors and expertise of graphic trainers. At the end of session we give them certificates in the soft form.
