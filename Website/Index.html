Introduction

Junoon Pakistan has been first established on 16 Nov 2019, our motive “Exuberance must not be compromised but must be glorified”. Junoon E Pakistan is a non-profit organization and a volunteering platform that encourages youth about different events occurring all across Pakistan. We welcome all to participate and polish their skills to gain experience and enhance their expertise. We expresses our views through graphics, video graphy, content writings, posters, social media team and various other events are arranged. Various activities and events have been organized under the banner of Junoon E Pakistan since its inception. Team Junoon is fully organized to further the legacy we have achieved so far. Before we dream of a perfect Pakistan, we need to address issues of our young generation and bring our youth to a common platform. Junoon E Pakistan is a new platform consisting of loyal and diligent workers working passionately to ensure the society’s welfare and growth and to support Pakistan in every possible manner. We offer training workshops and seminars, give job opportunities and certified internships, educate photoshop, illustrator and premier pro and impart leadership and management skills and public speaking. We give chance to every individual to work in a challenging environment. So we grant the exposure, you can’t get anywhere. Our youth consist of following institutes.
